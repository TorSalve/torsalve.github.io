Tor-Salve Dalsgaard

University of Copenhagen

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen.I am interested in users experiences and interactions with haptic technologies. These technologies have great potential to impact how humans approach their daily life. I want to engage with the human users of these technologies through user studies, workshops, hackathons and the like to understand how users want to use novel technologies and what kind experiences can be enabled through interactive technology.
Currently, I am trying to figure out how users want to interact with haptics and how the haptic experiences work. Take a look at my publications to see what I have got going on.
