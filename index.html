Tor-Salve Dalsgaard

Human-Centred Computing, Department of Computer Science, University of Copenhagen

I am a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen. My interests are within user experience of virtual reality and haptics. My research focuses on creating tools for designers and researchers, enabeling creation of novel interaction methods and techniques.
Currently, I am trying to figure out how users want to interact with mid-air haptics. Take a look at my publications to see what I have got going on. 🤔
